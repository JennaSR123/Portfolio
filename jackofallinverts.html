Jack of all Inverts - 2024

Jack of all Inverts is an invertebrate curator and small business owner who needed a quick setup for sales listings and husbandry information. I repurposed the template of this portfolio website to work for them at minimal cost. The business owner is very familiar with HTML and CSS and has been the most pleasant client to work with to date. She and I meet on a weekly basis to discuss branding design and make content changes to the website. I believe that her business needs are quickly outgrowing my basic services, and will need to move from raw HTML hosted on github to a more permanent shop solution that includes payment processing. For now, though, she is pleased with the design.
